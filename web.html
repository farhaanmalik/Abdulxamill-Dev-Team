Our Web Application Development Process

Consulting and Research

Our business development team members who have years of industry experience, will carefully assess your requirements and come up with a reasonable quote for your web application development project. Our expert team will also conduct comprehensive research of your business setup, in order to minimize technical and market risks in the entire software development lifecycle.

Front-end Development

Our project developers will precipitate your requirements deep into their minds and will work for hand in hand with developers in achieving that end. Our team begins by creating an intuitive front end to your application that is UI and UX design. We will also create a UX that will be uniform throughout the application and add both effects and animations without them hindering the application’s performance.

Back end Development

We design the core part of your application keeping in mind the availability, robustness, and a host of other essential features. We design a robust architecture so we can append new features in the future, and be highly flexible so as to be able to seamlessly integrate third-party services. Our developers also take security measures to protect user data and use advanced data structures to provide proper functionality.

Testing and Release

Our experienced testers will test every functionality and feature within your web application. The team will conduct multiple cycles of testing to ensure your web application works at the highest performance, with all the major browsers of all the platforms you have chosen. Our testing team also works hand in hand with the development team to eliminate major bugs and errors within your application.
